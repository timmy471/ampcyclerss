We are a Waste Recycling and Social Benefit Venture

Ampcycleres is Nigeria’s foremost waste recycling and social benefit venture that motivate post-consumers to recycle by creating value from their everyday waste.

We have developed a POINT-BASED incentive model with which we collect Pure Water Sachets (PWS), PET Plastic Bottles (PET), Used Beverage Cans (UBC), Glass bottles (GBS), Old Newspapers (ONP) and Brown Corrugated Cartons (BCC) from registered post-consumers and in turn reward them with POINTS, which when accrued can be used to redeem household items and cash.

Points are allocated based on a Points Earning Chart where quantity of items are equated to the amount of points earned.
